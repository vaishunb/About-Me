About Project

Overview of the Project.

This project Online admission form is used for students to submit the admission form through online. So they don’t need to be in a long queue. Then the staff can handle the data base easily. This project is so easy for sorting and short listing. When the Staff enter into this Admin then only they can view the database and download the spreadsheet directly. This will be handled by Administrators only.

  1.           Staff can view the students details who have applied for admission.
  2.           Staff can download the students details who have applied for admission.

When the Students want to apply their admission form for joining in our college, they have to enter into the Student then only they can view the form. There four forms are provided. They are,
  1.           UG Regular
  2.           UG Self-Finance
  3.           PG Regular
  4.           PG Self-Finance.
After submit their form Thank you page will be redirected.

Features

Features of the Project.

1. Admin

The person who has an administrator power that person only can access this part. After clicking this then the Login page will be displayed. After getting successful Login, they can view and download the database.

2. Students

The students can access this only. They don’t have the power to access the Administrators part. Because that Admin has the full security with the User Name and password. After clicking this Student Button they can view the available Graduates. There are,

  1.           UG Regular
  2.           UG Self-Finance
  3.           PG Regular
  4.           PG Self-Finance.

The students can apply for their Graduation using this forms. After giving the full form details then they will allow to submit their admission form. Then the Thank you page will be redirected. The Mail will be sent to their mail address.

3.Mail Send

After clicking the submit button, the form will be submitted automatically into the database. For the students confirmation, mail will be sent automatically with their admission number. This module has been done by using Google App Script.
